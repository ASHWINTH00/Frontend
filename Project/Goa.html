Welcome to Goa 🌊

The Beach Paradise for sun, sand & seafood


Goa is famous for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood. It's India's top beach destination that offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

Here's everything you need to enjoy this coastal paradise!

Quick Facts

  • Population: 1.5 million
  • Language: Konkani
  • Currency: Indian Rupee (₹)
  • Time Zone: IST (GMT +5:30)

Top Attractions

  1. Calangute Beach 🏖
  2. Basilica of Bom Jesus ⛪
  3. Dudhsagar Waterfalls 🌊
  4. Anjuna Flea Market 🛍
  5. Fort Aguada 🏰

Best Months to Visit Goa

Month Weather Activities
November - February Cool & Dry Beach parties, Sightseeing
March - May Hot & Dry Water sports, Sunbathing
June - October Monsoon Season Indoor activities, Spa retreats
